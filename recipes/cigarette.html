How to make propper cigarette for breakfest?

is making a cigarette hard ?

not really

dont buy cigarretes, remember making your owns is much cheaper, and can be even pretty entertaining!

all we will need to make a proper one:

  • some fine tobacco(for e.g. pueblo virginia
  • rolling papers
  • cotton filters
  • some patience for the process

it won't be easy at start, but soo you will be making cigaretess faster than 1minute

lets get into it:

grab your rolling paper, put some tobacco into it, add filter

now you just have to feel the rolling motion with your thumbs and index fingers, and boom! lick this bad boy, make it stic, and enjoy smoking!
