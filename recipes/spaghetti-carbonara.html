Doomer's spaghetti carbonara

Why is this carbonara the best?

this carbonara is the best because it's origin is from italian chef Vincenzo,

although it has been changed a little, so it's more suitable for slavic people,

the ones who are just like Doomer.

anyways, how can it be made?

it's easy so just chill out and prepare ingridients:

Ingridients list right here:

  1. Truly italic spaghetti(300g)
  2. 3 eggs
  3. 200 grams of pecorino/grana padano/parmigiano chesse
  4. for those who like to be fancy: guanciale
  5. for those like boomer: porkbelly
  6. salt and for the best result fresh grinded pepper, anyway regular pregrinded one will work

Here is how it's made:

  • cook your pasta in large pot(around 7 min for aldente) and dont hesitate with salt
  • mix your eggs well with a whisker or fork in a bowl
  • get a pan, heat it up and drop your guanciale/porkbelly in it without oil
  • add chesse and pepper(a lot of pepper) into your eggs, mix it up
  • put some pasta water to your mixture
  • take pasta straight from the pot to your pan with meat on it
  • add mixture and mix it up on low heat for few minutes
  • be careful with mixing it, because we dont want egg proteins to colapse
  • your pasta is ready so plate it gently and add fresh grinded pepper and some more chesse
